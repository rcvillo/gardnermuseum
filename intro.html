Isabella Stewart Gardner

Isabella Stewart Gardner was a wealthy art connoisseur and world traveler, who collaborated with "important art patrons and advisors" (gardnermuseum.org). Isabella curated her own museum which served the function of providing a haven to house her carefully chosen collection of "master & decorative arts", accumulated throughout her three decades abroad (gardnermuseum.org). Therefore, Isabella made sure the museum was built "to her own specifications" (Gardner Museum Map), with the interior architecture of Gardner's house clearly reflecting motifs she came across in her travels.
